Ilst. The capitol of the kingdom of Rolstavillian last in contention to the High Throne. King Rolst Ilstavillian, current ruler and King west of the Great River was a usurper of a forgotten house who's name was magically erased from all memory in all the land.

Early on Ilst contended to be a challenge to the throne after the kingdom was split in two by Krieg the Scourge.

The carnage of the two monster wars of the last centuries and decades of civil war between the three kingdoms has left most of Rolst desolate an in ruin.

Its borders stretch from the coast of the Serpant river in the east to the Fence Mountain range to the south and west.
